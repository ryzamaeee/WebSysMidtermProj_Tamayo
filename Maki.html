About


"Kailan?" by Maki was such a heart-wrenching ballad about how badly one misses the person and the ache of missing the love. It talked of the feeling of leaving the other person behind; one asks questions like, "When did you feel that you no longer loved me? What if you felt it never at all?" The words would bring out the question that one would have over this sudden change in terms of a relationship and his longings for answers on what went wrong. The lyrics are relatable to anyone who has ever been through the bittersweet ache of unrequited love, reminding us that even the most cherished memories can fade with time.
