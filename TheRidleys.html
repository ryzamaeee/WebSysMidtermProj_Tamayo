About


"Aphrodite" by The Ridleys is a love song that brings its subject into the position of a goddess. With lyrics such as "You're the moon that glows in the sky / Lighting up the world when it's blue," one gets a picture of beauty and power. The singer is totally infatuated, saying, "I see the galaxies when I look in your eyes," and declaring, "I swear to Zeus you're Aphrodite in disguise." "Aphrodite" captures the wonder and the awe of being deeply in love, with the person you love as one in a million.
