About


"Nothing," a song by Bruno Major: warm, comfortable intimacy in a relationship, to rejoice in small moments. Movie nights, video game evenings, just enjoying each other's company. There is comfort and contentment found by the lyrics "There's nothing like doing nothing with you" in just sitting there, or having the silly conversations. Sometimes it is the unplanned and effortless moments-full of intimacy-that most touch the heart.
