About


The song "Ikot" by Over October is basically about the fear of losing something or someone special. The song really feels like being stuck in some kind of admiration and indecision, unsure whether admitting your feelings would spoil that bond you share. The vocalist sings, "Aaminin ko ba o baka bigla lang mawala / Kung ano mang pumapagitan sa 'ting dalawa?" or in english translation, "Should I confess or will everything we have disappear?". "Ikot" is one of those songs that everyone who has ever been afraid to take a chance because they're scared of 'ruining' a beautiful friendship for having feelings, can relate to.
