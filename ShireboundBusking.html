About


"Waltz of Four Left Feet" by Shirebound And Busking is a song about settling for less and deceiving oneself that he or she is content with what he or she has. In truth, there's something inside that screams out for more. The words of the song really echo how hard it is to accept a situation, like singing, "Sapat na sa 'kin ang ganito oh, ang pagmasdan ka sa malayo" which means This is enough for me, to watch you from afar. This song addresses the inner struggle one feels when wanting something that cannot be reached, but then convincing oneself that one does not need it-something one really wants. It is that anthem of a person who has settled for less and wished for the strength to dance to another drum.
