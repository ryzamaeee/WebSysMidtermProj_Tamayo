About


"Backburner" by Niki is a national anthem for anyone who's ever been the second choice. In essence, the song captures bittersweet feelings when somebody else is one's "backup plan," singing to themselves, "Maybe I'm just not better than this, I haven't tried / 'Cause maybe you'll finally choose me after you've had more time." In the lines, one can imagine always waiting for somebody to finally choose them again and then be on this emotional rollercoaster with the whole being on the backburner. This is the song for anyone who has ever been made to feel irrelevant yet still held onto a little hope that someday their time would come.
