About


"Fallingforyou" by The 1975 is not only a love song, but rather it's a truthful presentation of the vulnerability attached to falling for someone. "I think I'm falling, I'm falling for you," as sung by the band, captures the uncertainty and thrill of being in new love. It reflects longing to be together, thrill at stealing away, and hope that maybe their feelings are mutual. It's a reminder that even in the midst of doubt, the possibility of love is a beautiful and powerful thing.
