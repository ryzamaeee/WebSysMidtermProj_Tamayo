About


"Tila Tala" by Syd Hartha is a love song for a hopeless romantic and someone who falls head-over-heels in love for someone who is already lost. The title itself was in the form of "Tila Tala," wherein "tila" could be translated to meaning like a star, referring to the feeling of pining for someone that nobody seemed to be able to approach. Poet and singer Syd Hartha's haunting song on unrequited love evokes a vivid image on paper with such lines: "Tila tala sa kalangitan, kaakit-akit subalit ako'y hanggang tingin lang" which means, Like a star in the sky, so captivating, but I can only look.
