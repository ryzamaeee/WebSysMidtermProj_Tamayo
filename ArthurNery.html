About


The song "Life Puzzle" by Arthur Nery is about the intricate link between two people expressing and exploring their feelings for one another. The issues brought out are, therefore, issues like vulnerability and love, healing and can relate to nearly anyone who has ever thought and wished for a complete connection. Urgent and passionate, it's calling the listener near to share the heart. In this complicated world where relationships often complicate life, "Life Puzzle" reminds finding that last piece to make life complete.
