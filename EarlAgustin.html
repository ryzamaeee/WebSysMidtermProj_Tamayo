About


This song, "Dalangin" by Earl Agustin, is actually a beautiful declaration of love and commitment. It was about finding that person with whom you could feel a destiny, those eyes captured you, and their presence filled you with longing to be with him. Lyrics say a deep desire, to hold his hand, to share a life together, and to find your second home.

The title "Dalangin" means "prayer", and it paints the picture of how intensely this person is desired with love by the speaker. This is a prayer, but not only for love, for having this person outlive him.

The melodic tone here has been gentle and romantic, like the sensitivity expressed in the lines. It is a love song that speaks about the power of love and hope for forever.
