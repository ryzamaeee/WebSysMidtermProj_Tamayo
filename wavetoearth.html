About


"annie." by Wave to Earth is a song dedicated to all people who ever were forced inside the societal box. Title "annie" is taken from Korean "ani", "which means "no" and represents the point of this song - telling society to turn it down and take yourself instead. The lyrics are a brave declaration against the superficial, singing, "I don't belong with big names / Nor do I care 'bout fancy numbers / And I don't like jewels / It doesn't shine on my fingers." "annie." is a reminder that happiness lies within and not in outer validation.
